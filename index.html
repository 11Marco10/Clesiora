About Celsiora

An Investment Fund guided by passion and hard work

Celsiora Investment Management is a venture led by 4 students coming from top universities across Europe and passionate about financial markets. We adopt a disciplined bottom-up strategy centered on companies' intrinsic value and fundamental analysis. We aim at bringing fresh ideas and perspective over various industries in order to deliver the best value to our fund and share our vision through our network.​
