About Celsiora

Building Financial Legacies Since 2005

Celsiora Investment Management is an independent investment firm specializing in bespoke portfolio management for high-net-worth individuals, families, and institutions.

With offices in New York, London, and Singapore, we leverage global insights to deliver exceptional risk-adjusted returns through disciplined investment processes and rigorous research.

Our team of seasoned professionals brings decades of experience across diverse market cycles, enabling us to navigate complexity and identify unique opportunities for our clients.
